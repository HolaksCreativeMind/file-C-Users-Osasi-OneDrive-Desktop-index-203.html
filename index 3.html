Welcome to HOLAKS CREATIVE MINDS INSTITUTE

We are on a mission to empower youths with relevant digital, creative, and vocational skills that can help them succeed in life and contribute meaningfully to society.

About Us

HOLAKS CREATIVE MINDS INSTITUTE was founded out of a passion to equip young people with practical knowledge and life-changing skills. We aim to raise a generation of young minds who are creative, independent, and purpose-driven.

Our vision is to become a hub for youth empowerment through ICT, vocational education, and entrepreneurship training in our community and beyond.

We believe that every young person has potential, and our goal is to help them discover and develop it.

Our Programs

  • Graphics Design (CorelDRAW, Canva, Photoshop Basics)
  • Paint Production & Entrepreneurship
  • Career Development and Digital Literacy
